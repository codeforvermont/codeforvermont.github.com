CodeAcross Waterbury

Vermont Center for Geographic Information (VCGI) is hosting a low-key CodeAcross Waterbury event in solidarity with the CodeAcross events being held across the world during the weekend of February 21-23.

Coders, nonprofits looking for civic hacking help (or interested in finding out what that means!), and GIS geeks can join us from noon to 1PM on Saturday, February 22 for an hour of free pizza and appreciation of Open Data! VCGI has been openly providing free geospatial data to the masses for decades, and we want to see more (even non-geospatial)!
